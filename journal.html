The Journal — Volume XIV

Dispatches
from the Maison.

Long-form writing on craft, culture and the quiet chronology of our fourteen-year pursuit.

Origin — 01

The California Manuscript.

California is not a place; it is a pacing. The rhythm of the coast — from the salt-grey mornings of Venice to the dry gold of the Valley — taught us the most important thing a maker can learn: that the material always knows more than the hand. Presidential began in 2012 because a small group of Californians noticed that cannabis, once it crossed a certain threshold of care, began to behave less like a commodity and more like a vintage.

The first Moon Rock was assembled, not manufactured. It was the product of a particular patience — flower chosen for density, distillate chosen for clarity, kief chosen for its trichome character — and a refusal to rush any of the three. Fourteen years later, the patience is the only thing that has not changed.

Craft — 02

Why We Abandoned Tobacco.

The Blunt, as most people know it, is a vehicle for nicotine. We found that idea at odds with what we were doing — why build a more perfect cannabis, then wrap it in a leaf that worked against it? Our Blunt is 100% hemp. Tobacco-free. It burns at a slower, cooler rate and lets the terpenes of the flower speak in their own register.

A small change, perhaps, but those are the changes that compound over years. A cigar maker would recognize the logic: the wrapper is never just a wrapper.

Atelier — 03

Three Strains, One Estate.

The collaboration with THC Design began, as all good ones do, with a conversation over a bench. We share a simple belief: that single-estate flower, grown with obsessive attention, expresses more than any blend can. The Atelier releases — XJ-13, 24K and Gorilla Goo — are the proof of that belief. Each is single-sourced, strain-specific from flower to kief, and released in strictly limited quantity.

We do not guarantee they will return in the same form. That is the nature of an estate.

Questions — 04

A Quiet FAQ.

What is a Moon Rock?

A Moon Rock is top-shelf organic flower that has been infused with premium THC distillate so that it permeates the bud to the stem, then hand-coated in the highest-quality kief. It is the form we introduced to the American market in 2012.

Why is it called "The World's Strongest Preroll"?

Because our preroll contains 1.5 grams of infused Moon Rock — not conventional ground flower. Between the flower, the distillate infused throughout, and the kief coating, the resulting potency is in a class of its own.

Is the Blunt really tobacco-free?

Entirely. Our wrap is 100% hemp — no tobacco, no nicotine. It burns slowly and cleanly and lets the flower speak in its own register.

What is the Atelier Edition?

A haute collaboration with THC Design — estate-grown, single-sourced, strain-specific Moon Rocks (XJ-13, 24K, Gorilla Goo), made in strictly limited quantities.

Where can I find Presidential?

Through curated licensed dispensaries across California. Our definitive locator is maintained on Weedmaps, reachable from the Dispensaries page.

Do you ship direct to consumers?

No. In accordance with California law, Presidential is available exclusively through our licensed retail partners.
