About the House

Home of the World's Strongest Cannabis.

Welcome to Presidential, home of the world's strongest cannabis products. We are committed to bringing the most innovative cannabis products to the market, with a focus on a standard of excellence, reliability and customer service.

At Presidential, we are renowned for our Infused Moon Rock Prerolls — known as "The World's Strongest Prerolls" — as well as our Infused Moon Rocks, which we introduced to the market in 2012. Considered one of the founding fathers of the infused product market in the United States of America, we take pride in continuing to lead product innovation with the introduction of our revolutionary Infused Moon Rock Blunts. Our Blunt wrap is 100% tobacco free.

Founded on the American spirit of entrepreneurship, our goal at Presidential is to combine organically grown cannabis with cutting-edge science and technology to create the safest, most reliable, and innovative products on the market. We have proven that great entrepreneurs and teams are made through experiences, hard work, and inspiration. We now serve customers all over the state of California.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we do.

Legal — Terms of Engagement

Customer Care, Privacy & Wholesale.

Customer Care. Our concierge team is available to assist with product questions, dispensary availability, and any matter pertaining to your experience with the Maison. Reach us via the Concierge form and expect a thoughtful, personal reply.

Privacy & Safety. We treat your information with the same discretion with which we treat our craft. Data shared with Presidential is used solely for fulfilling your request — never sold, never listed.

Wholesale & Hospitality. For dispensary partnerships and hospitality programs, contact our wholesale liaison through the Concierge page. Partnerships are curated personally and confirmed in writing.

Payment. Purchase of Presidential products takes place through licensed retail dispensary partners. Accepted methods — cash, debit, and digital wallet — vary by retailer and jurisdiction. Presidential does not transact directly with end consumers.

Logistics

Distribution & Returns.

Presidential is distributed exclusively through licensed California dispensary partners. As such, purchase, transport, and returns are governed by the policies of the retailer that served you. For matters of quality, consistency, or authenticity of any Presidential product, contact our Concierge and we will make it right — always.

Wholesale shipments to our dispensary partners are scheduled weekly and coordinated by our distribution team. Atelier Edition releases are hand-allocated to a curated list of partners.
