The Manifesto

Single-Estate. Single-Strain.

The Atelier Edition is the private expression of the Maison — produced in strictly limited quantity. Each batch is single-sourced from award-winning cultivator THC Design's estate. The flower is never blended; the distillate is strain-specific; the kief is hand-pressed from the same family of trichomes.

Three strains, released together. Chosen to honor the California terroir: one Sativa-forward, one balanced, one Indica-anchored.
